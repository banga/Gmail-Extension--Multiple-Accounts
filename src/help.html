Why can't I add account login details anymore?
  • In previous versions of this extension, you could add username and password for each account, so that you did not have to sign in to view emails. However, this frequently caused the extension to confuse some accounts, because Gmail remembers your signed-in details differently from the authentication details for this extension. Since this extension uses your Gmail pages to retrieve contents of emails, it would retrieve the wrong emails or just fail.
    This leads to bad experiences, so I decided to simply use the signed in accounts which are more reliable, instead of putting users at risk.
Why do I see so many extra emails?
  • This extension can now synchronize emails from multiple labels in your gmail accounts. By default, I added the 'Important' label so that users can discover this feature. This synchronizes all emails in your account marked as Important, even the ones you may have archived. You can get rid of any labels in the Options page.
What are some features I don't know about?
  • You can synchronize multiple labels. Go to Options to configure. Apart from the labels you defined, these can be special labels such as Important, Chat or Starred.
  • Pressing Tab focuses emails and checkboxes. When an email is focused, press
    j to focus the next email
    k to focus the previous email
    Enter to collpase or expand the email's contents
    x to select/deselect the email
  • You can shift-click on an email to select it
  • You can drag over emails to select them
  • When an email is open, if you click on the gray area next to the buttons, the email will close up. This is useful if the email is too long and you don't want to go all the way up.
